## Short Intro

`company-growth-check-in` is a reusable AI skill for one question: is this company or role still worth staying in, and am I still getting meaningful growth from staying?

It uses an agent-led check-in flow:

- bootstrap context about the company, role, and goals
- run daily or ad hoc question-led reviews
- save dated reports for weekly and monthly synthesis

The goal is not generic journaling. The goal is better career judgment over time.

## Launch Post

I built `company-growth-check-in`, a reusable AI skill for a question a lot of people have but rarely structure well:

Is this company or role still worth staying in, and am I still getting meaningful growth from staying?

It uses a simple loop:

- bootstrap the company and role context
- let the agent ask focused questions one at a time
- write dated reports
- synthesize them into weekly or monthly reviews

The point is not loyalty or generic journaling. The point is clearer career decisions from repeated evidence.
